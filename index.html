About

Hi there. This is Kaz, a junior Web Developper based in NYC, born and grewup in Japan.

Experienced as a business owner who produced and sold bags. In this business, I covered a wide range such as marketing, website management, task management, self-management, problem-solving ability, supply management, information gathering, design, sewing, production, office work, and customer service - Whole business. In particular, I have been improving the efficiency of office work since the company was established, and I have streamlined the business by creating customer management applications using FileMaker and accounting using automatic spreadsheet calculations.

After creating an in-house system with FileMaker, I wanted to make the sales channel an original shopping site, so I created it myself by combining no-code tool (Squarespace) and plug-ins such as payment services and mailers. As no-code isn’t flexible and limited to what I wanted to do, gradually I became more eager to buildup everything myself.

I switched focus from own business to engineering because of covid. I started studying coding in English but I was confused with the language. Decided to go back to my country to take a coding boot camp in Japanese then came back to NYC, and took another coding boot camp in English. Learned not only coding - I’ve learned communication and culture are different than where I used to be.

Graduating in May 2022, excited and looking forward to meeting people and entering the tech industry as a web developper.
